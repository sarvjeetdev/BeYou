Important Security Information

You are about to generate cryptographic keys for message signing and encryption.

  • Your public keys will be stored on the server and shared with other users.
  • Your private keys will be shown to you once and must be saved securely.
  • Private keys will never be stored on the server after this page.
  • If you lose your private keys, you won't be able to decrypt messages sent to you.

In a real-world application, key generation would happen client-side in your browser for better security.
